The History of Football Factory

FotbollsFabriken stands as one of the world's leading football retailers, catering to football players and enthusiasts with top-tier products since 1995. Apart from our flagship stores in the heart of Paris, central Copenhagen, and Stockholm, we also operate locally-tailored online shops in Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Finland, the Netherlands, Germany, France, Austria. We proudly deliver to every corner of the globe through FotbollsFactorystore.com.

The CEO of Football Factory

Coday Awahmed is the visionary leader at the helm of Football Factory, one of the world's premier football retailers. With a distinguished career in the sports retail industry, Coday has successfully steered the company to new heights, consistently demonstrating a deep passion for the sport and an unwavering commitment to excellence. A true football enthusiast, Coday has been a driving force behind Football Factory's growth and reputation since taking the reins. Under Coday's leadership, the company has solidified its position as a global leader in the market, offering football players and fans access to top-quality merchandise and unparalleled customer service.
